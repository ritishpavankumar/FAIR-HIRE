About FairHire

Smart talent matching for the modern workplace

Our Mission

FairHire aims to eliminate hiring bias by focusing purely on skills, qualifications, and fit. We believe the best candidate should rise to the top based on merit.

How It Works

Our matching algorithm evaluates candidates based on skills, projects, experience, and assessment scores to calculate a fair fit score.

Privacy First

Your data stays yours. We don't share your information with third parties. All profile data is stored locally.

For Everyone

Whether you're a student looking for your first job or a recruiter seeking top talent, FairHire provides a level playing field.
